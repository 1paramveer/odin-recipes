Coffee (Instant)

Instant coffee is a convenient and quick way to enjoy a cup of coffee. It is made from coffee beans that have been roasted, ground, and brewed, then processed into a dry powder or granules. To make instant coffee, you simply need to dissolve the coffee powder in hot water.

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee powder or granules
  • Hot water
  • Optional: sugar, milk, or cream to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water: Heat water to a desired temperature. It is recommended to use water that is just below boiling point, around 200°F (93°C).
  2. Measure the coffee: Determine the desired strength of your coffee. Start with approximately 1 teaspoon of instant coffee per 8 ounces (240 ml) of water. Adjust the amount based on your preference.
  3. Add coffee to a cup: Scoop the desired amount of instant coffee into your cup.
  4. Pour hot water: Carefully pour the hot water into the cup, directly over the instant coffee.
  5. Stir: Use a spoon to stir the mixture vigorously, ensuring the coffee granules fully dissolve in the hot water.
  6. Optional: Add sugar, milk, or cream to taste. Stir well to incorporate.
  7. Enjoy: Your instant coffee is now ready to be enjoyed! Sip and savor the rich aroma and flavor.
